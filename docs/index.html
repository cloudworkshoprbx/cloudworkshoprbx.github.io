Welcome to the Delta Line Project website!

Delta Line Project is a game in Roblox based on Hong Kong. In the game, there are 3 automatic lines connecting the region. Players are well served by a variety fleet of AI trains. Also, players can enjoy the view of the surrounding scenery using the front windows, enhancing the overall experience.
